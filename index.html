A Scholar-Proposed Solution

Some scholars propose a bridge fund that temporarily covers MSRS needs during processing delays.

  1. Form a core team and consult UPCM admin.
  2. Design fund disbursement protocols (e.g. based on need).
  3. Propose guidelines (e.g. limit amount per scholar).
  4. Partner with UPCM alumni or orgs for seed funding.
  5. Ensure liquidation tracking and transparency.

This guide does not endorse or solicit donations. It merely illustrates peer-led solutions.
